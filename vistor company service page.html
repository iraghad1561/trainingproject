Why using ‘Training Place'!.

Applicants have many difficulties finding the appropriate company for the training by traditional ways,
due to the lack of knowledge of where to find the training offers. which affects the process of choosing
and determine the suitable training and company.
“Training place” website aim to support the companies that provide training,
by enabling the company to add their training offers on the list of training offers in order to make it reach
a largest number of intersted applicants.
It provides flexibility for the registered company to view applicant’s profiles to choose the appropriate applicant according to the company’s needs, to send him a training request. By introducing companies to those qualified applicants will help companies to create a more efficient and productive work environment.
